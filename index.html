Understanding The Service

What is Social Media Management?

Social media management is the strategic planning, creation, publishing and optimisation of content across social media platforms to grow your brand, generate leads and build long-term credibility.

At ProAgri Media, we don't just post content — we build structured digital strategies that position your business in front of the right agricultural audience.

Our Process

How it WORKS

Our structured process ensures your content is strategic, professionally produced, and aligned with your business goals, before it ever goes live.

1

Strategy & Focus Session

We meet with you to identify your key focus points, goals, target audience and priority products or services. This forms the foundation of your monthly content plan.

2

Content Creation

Our editorial and design teams develop professional visuals, written content and platform-specific messaging tailored to your brand and the agricultural market.

3

Client Review & Approval

All content is shared with you for review. We refine where needed and ensure everything aligns with your expectations before publishing.

4

Campaign & Scheduling

Once approved, our social media team launches structured campaigns, schedules posts strategically and manages ongoing engagement and enquiries.
